Frequently asked questions

Here are some of our most popular questions. You can also ask us questions on the contact us panel below!

1. How much does it cost to use Rocket Rabbit?

Its absolutley free! there will be ZERO cost to users of Rocket Rabbit. You dont even use your own money to invest, we've got you covered.

2. What if i dont know anything about stocks?

Great! we dont expect you to, the main purpose of Rocket Rabbit is to teach you! The stock market has this perception of large entry barriers for new investors when it really shouldnt be hard at all!

3. How does Rocket Rabbit make money?

We make money from ad revenue through our paper trading app (coming soon). Later down the line when we develop a portfolio which you can invest real money into, we will charge yearly fees depending on your total stock balance.

4. When can i download Rocket Rabbit?!

Soon! we are still developing our mobile app for both the App Store & Google Play Store. You can express your interest for our upcoming alpha release below and also be the first to know when we go live!
